Reflection and Insights

Wordpress was extremely easy to develop for, and offered a quick and easy way of customising the theme, as well as an intuitive design of Wordpress that makes it simple to create webpages for. Joomla, however, was difficult to learn the basics for, as I took more time trying to learn how to customise and develop a page for my website than it did to actually design and develop my webpages. Joomla doesn't seem user-friendly, but once each step of making a website with Joomla was made clear, it was just as easy to perform the tasks as it was in Wordpress. I think Wordpress is much more user friendly and easier to develop for, but once Joomla is figured out, it becomes easier to develop with HTML than it does in Wordpress.
